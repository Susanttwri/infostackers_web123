PAS - Account Deletion Instructions

How to Delete Your Account and Data

  1. Log in to the PAS app.
  2. Open the menu by tapping the menu icon.
  3. Go to the "My Account" section.
  4. Scroll down to the bottom of the screen.
  5. Tap the "Delete" icon to initiate account deletion.
  6. Confirm your choice when prompted.
  7. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 48 hours.

Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be removed from our systems.
